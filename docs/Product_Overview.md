# Bloom & Grow Marketing Planner
## Product Overview

---

### Executive Summary

The Bloom & Grow Marketing Planner is a comprehensive B2B marketing management platform designed specifically for multi-brand, multi-regional marketing teams. This cloud-based solution streamlines content creation workflows, campaign management, and team collaboration across social media, email marketing, events, and task management.

---

### The Challenge

Regional marketing teams managing multiple brands face significant operational challenges:

- **Fragmented Tools**: Teams juggle multiple platforms for social scheduling, email campaigns, event planning, and task management
- **Workflow Bottlenecks**: Content creation involves designers, copywriters, and publishers with no clear handoff process
- **Regional Coordination**: Duplicating successful campaigns across regions requires manual effort and introduces errors
- **Visibility Gaps**: Managers lack real-time visibility into content pipelines and team workloads
- **Asset Chaos**: Marketing assets scattered across drives, emails, and cloud storage

---

### The Solution

The Bloom & Grow Marketing Planner consolidates marketing operations into a single, intuitive platform with role-based access control and automated workflow notifications.

---

### Core Features

#### 1. Unified Content Calendar
- Visual calendar view across all content types (social posts, emails, events)
- Filter by brand, region, platform, or team member
- Drag-and-drop scheduling with conflict detection
- Week, month, and agenda views

#### 2. Social Media Management
- Multi-platform support: TikTok, Instagram (Feed, Story, Reels), Facebook
- Campaign grouping and tagging
- Status tracking: Draft, Scheduled, Published
- Graphics workflow with designer/publisher assignments
- Content duplication across multiple regions with one click

#### 3. Email Campaign Management
- B2B email campaign planning and tracking
- Template management in asset library
- Status progression: Draft, Review, Scheduled, Sent
- Designer and publisher workflow integration
- Campaign performance notes and metrics tracking

#### 4. Events & Promotions
- Trade shows, product launches, training sessions, and promotions
- Event type categorization with visual indicators
- Multi-region event coordination
- Promotion details and special offer tracking
- Venue, time, and attendee management

#### 5. Task Management
- Kanban-style board with customizable columns
- Priority levels (Low, Medium, High, Urgent)
- Due date tracking with overdue alerts
- Team member assignment
- Task duplication across regions

#### 6. Asset Library
- Centralized storage for all marketing assets
- Categories: Graphics, Data Files, Email Templates, Videos, Documents
- Brand-specific folder organization
- Tagging and search functionality
- Version tracking

#### 7. Workflow Automation & Notifications
- **In-App Notifications**: Real-time alerts for workflow updates
- **Slack Integration**: Direct message notifications to team members
- **Automated Triggers**:
  - Designer notified when content is created
  - Publisher notified when graphics are marked complete
  - Task assignee notified on assignment

#### 8. Team & Role Management
- Role-based access: Administrator, Manager, User
- Regional team assignments
- Designer and publisher workflow roles
- User activity tracking

#### 9. Multi-Brand Support
- Pre-configured brand profiles with color coding
- Brand-specific asset organization
- Cross-brand campaign coordination
- Visual brand indicators throughout interface

#### 10. Multi-Region Operations
- Supported regions: ANZ, Singapore, Malaysia, Indonesia, China, Hong Kong
- One-click content duplication to multiple regions
- Regional filtering and views
- Cross-region campaign coordination
