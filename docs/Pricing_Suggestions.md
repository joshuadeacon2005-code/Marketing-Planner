# Bloom & Grow Marketing Planner
## Pricing Strategy & Recommendations

---

### Pricing Model Options

#### Option A: Per-User Subscription (Recommended)

This model scales with team size and is easy for customers to understand.

| Tier | Users | Monthly Price | Annual Price (20% discount) |
|------|-------|---------------|----------------------------|
| Starter | Up to 5 users | $149/month | $1,430/year |
| Professional | Up to 15 users | $349/month | $3,350/year |
| Business | Up to 30 users | $599/month | $5,750/year |
| Enterprise | Unlimited | Custom | Custom |

**All tiers include:**
- All core features
- Unlimited brands
- Unlimited regions
- Asset library storage (tiered)
- Email support

**Storage allocations:**
- Starter: 10 GB
- Professional: 50 GB
- Business: 200 GB
- Enterprise: Unlimited

---

#### Option B: Per-Seat Pricing

Simple, linear pricing that scales directly with headcount.

| User Type | Monthly Price | Annual Price |
|-----------|---------------|--------------|
| Admin | $49/user/month | $470/user/year |
| Manager | $39/user/month | $374/user/year |
| User | $29/user/month | $278/user/year |

**Minimum commitment:** 3 seats
**Volume discounts:** 10% off for 20+ seats, 20% off for 50+ seats

### Pricing Psychology Tips

1. **Anchor high:** Show Enterprise pricing first in sales conversations
2. **Highlight value:** Compare to cost of multiple separate tools
3. **Annual discount:** 20% encourages longer commitments
4. **Free trial:** 14-day trial to demonstrate value
5. **Onboarding included:** Reduces perceived risk
