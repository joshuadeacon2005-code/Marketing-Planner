What is the Marketing Planner?

The Bloom & Grow Marketing Planner is a centralised B2B platform built to unify every part of the marketing workflow — from initial project creation through design, copywriting, approval, and publishing — across multiple brands and geographic regions.

🗂 Project Management
Organise campaigns into projects with deliverables, deadlines, and team assignments.
📅 Content Calendar
Visualise all social posts, emails, events, and tasks on a unified calendar.
✅ Approval Workflow
Route content through Design → Copywriting → Publishing with role-based approvals.
📊 Analytics & Performance
Track engagement metrics and top-performing posts per brand.
🖼 Asset Library
Store and manage brand assets — graphics, videos, templates — with Canva sync.
🔔 Smart Notifications
Automated in-app and Slack alerts for deadlines, assignments, and approvals.
ℹ️
Multi-Brand & Multi-Region

The planner is built to support multiple brands (e.g. different venue brands under the Bloom & Grow group) and multiple regions. Content, assets, and team members are all scoped to specific brands and regions so that each team only sees what is relevant to them.

Dashboard

The Dashboard is your command centre. It gives you a live snapshot of all marketing activity across every brand and region you have access to.

Stat Cards

The top row shows four glowing metric cards. Each card displays a count and a mini trend chart — hover over a card to see more detail. Clicking a card navigates to the relevant section.

📣 Social Posts
Total scheduled & published social media posts.
📧 Email Campaigns
Total email campaigns in progress or sent.
📅 Upcoming Events
Events scheduled within the upcoming period.

Unified Calendar

Below the stat cards is a full calendar showing all content types in one view — social posts, email campaigns, events, and tasks. Use the filter dropdowns above the calendar to narrow by brand, region, or content type. The calendar supports Month, Week, and Day views.

💡
Colour Coding

Calendar events are colour-coded by brand colour, making it easy to see at a glance which brand each piece of content belongs to.

A summary of active projects is shown below the calendar, including progress bars and workflow stage. Click any project to open its detail panel.

Upcoming Deadlines

A list of items due within the next 7 days is displayed in the right panel, sorted by urgency. Items are colour-coded — overdue items appear in red, items due today in amber.

Recent Activity

A live feed showing the most recent actions taken by your team — new posts created, tasks completed, assets uploaded, approvals submitted, etc.

Creating a Project

Click "+ New Project"

The orange button in the top-right of the Projects page opens the project creation dialog.

Fill in project details

Enter a Project Name, optional description, select the Brand and Region, and optionally link to a Campaign. You can also set a date range and assign default team members (Designer, Copywriter, Publisher).

Add Deliverables

Select the deliverable types needed — e.g. Instagram Posts, Facebook Posts, EDM Graphics, Website Banners, Event Materials. Dimensions auto-fill for each type.

Save the project

The project is created and appears in your project list. Assigned team members receive a Slack notification with design specs and a direct "Create in Canva" link.

Viewing & Managing a Project

Click any project card to open the Project Detail Panel. Here you can:

  • See all deliverables and their current workflow stage
  • Click a deliverable to expand it and update its status
  • Add reference images for the designer
  • Mark stages as complete to advance the workflow
  • Edit project details or delete the project
💡
Canva Integration

When a Designer is assigned to a deliverable, they receive a Slack message with a "Create in Canva" button. This opens a new Canva project with the correct dimensions pre-set, saving time and reducing size errors.

Project Views

Switch between three views using the icons in the top-right of the Projects page:

  • List view — compact table with all projects
  • Card/Kanban view — grouped by workflow stage
  • Calendar view — projects plotted by date range
📤

Publishing Queue

The Publishing Queue shows all deliverables that have completed the Design and Copywriting stages and are now awaiting publication to their target social platform.

Use the Brand, Region, and Type filter dropdowns at the top to narrow the list to what you need to act on.

Publishing a Deliverable

Find the deliverable in the Queue

Use filters to narrow by brand, region, or content type. Each card shows the deliverable name, target platform, assigned publisher, and due date.

Click "Publish" or the arrow icon

This opens the Publish screen where you can review the final content, caption, and choose the platform account to publish to.

Select the connected account

Choose the Meta Page, TikTok account, LinkedIn page, or Twitter account to post to. Only connected accounts (configured in Settings) are shown.

Confirm & Publish

Click "Publish Now". The content is sent to the platform via API. On success, the deliverable moves to the Published section.

ℹ️
Platform Status

The queue page shows a platform status indicator for each integration (Meta, TikTok, LinkedIn, Twitter). A green tick means the integration is connected and ready. A grey icon means the platform is not yet configured — contact your Admin to set it up in Settings.

✅

Published

The Published page is the permanent record of all content that has been successfully published through the platform. It shows the post, platform, publish date, the team member who published it, and any available performance metrics.

Use this page to review what has gone out, cross-reference dates, and confirm that scheduled content was published as planned.

Tasks

The Tasks section is for standalone work items that don't fit within a Project — things like briefing meetings, research tasks, admin to-dos, or any action that needs tracking.

Task Views

Switch between two layouts:

  • Kanban Board — columns for To Do, In Progress, and Done. Drag and drop cards between columns.
  • List View — a table layout with sorting and filtering.

Creating a Task

Click "+ New Task"

The orange button in the top-right opens the task creation dialog.

Fill in the details

Enter a Title, optional description, select the Brand and Region, set a Due Date, assign a Priority (Low, Medium, High, Urgent), and assign it to a team member.

Save the task

The task appears on the board and the assigned person receives a notification.

Uploading an Asset

Click "+ Add Asset"

Opens the asset upload dialog.

Fill in details

Enter a name and description, select the Brand, choose the Asset Type, and add tags to make it searchable. You can also add an external URL (e.g. a Canva link, DreamPIM link, or Asset Portal link).

Upload the file or paste a link

Either upload a file directly or provide an external link. Canva designs can be synced automatically via the Canva integration.

Searching & Filtering

Use the search bar and filter dropdowns to find assets quickly:

  • Search bar — searches asset names and descriptions
  • Type filter — show only Graphics, Videos, Documents, etc.
  • Brand filter — show only assets for a specific brand
  • Tag filter — find assets by tag

Views

Switch between Grid view (thumbnail tiles) and List view (table) using the icons in the top-right.

Canva Sync

If Canva is connected (configured in Settings by your Admin), you can sync designs directly from your Canva account using the "Sync from Canva" button. This pulls the latest versions of your Canva designs into the asset library automatically.

Archive

The Archive (labelled "Archive" in the nav) stores completed and sent content — social posts that have been published, email campaigns that have been sent, and past events.

Use the Archive to reference past campaigns, review historic content, or find a previous post to duplicate for a new campaign. Filter by brand, region, content type, or date range to find specific items quickly.

ℹ️
Read-Only

Content in the Archive cannot be edited. To reuse a piece of content, use the Duplicate function to create a new editable copy.

Roles & Permissions

Every user is assigned one of three roles. Your role determines what you can see and do.

ADMIN

Full access to everything including Settings, user management, brand/region configuration, and all integrations. Can create, edit, and delete any content across all brands and regions.

MANAGER

Can view and manage content across all brands and regions. Can create/edit projects, posts, tasks, and assets. Cannot access Settings or manage users.

USER

Can view content within their assigned region/brand and update items assigned to them. Typically designers, copywriters, and coordinators who work on specific deliverables.

Approval Self-Prevention

The platform enforces a rule that you cannot approve your own content. If you created or submitted a piece of content, a different team member must approve it. This ensures quality control and a genuine review step.

Meta (Facebook & Instagram)

The Meta integration allows direct publishing of posts and reels to Facebook Pages and Instagram Business accounts, as well as retrieving analytics (reach, impressions, engagement). Setup is done in Settings → Integrations by an Admin.

TikTok, LinkedIn, Twitter/X

These integrations support publishing content directly from the planner. OAuth credentials are entered in Settings. Once connected, accounts appear in the Publishing Queue when publishing content for the relevant platform.

Canva

Two Canva features are available:

  • Create in Canva link — Designers receive a Slack notification with a "Create in Canva" button that opens a new design with correct dimensions pre-set.
  • Canva Sync — Connect your Canva account via OAuth in Settings to sync finished designs directly into the Asset Library.

Slack

Slack DM notifications are sent automatically for workflow events. The Slack integration is configured server-side. Contact your Administrator or developer if Slack notifications are not being received.

⚠️
Integration Tokens

API tokens and OAuth credentials for all integrations are stored securely on the server. Never share your platform credentials. If a token expires, the Admin will need to re-authenticate the integration in Settings.

Tips & Best Practices

Staying Organised

  • Always assign a Brand and Region to every piece of content — this ensures the right team sees the right work.
  • Use Projects to group related content. For example, one project per campaign or event.
  • Set deadlines on all deliverables so the automated reminder system can alert your team in advance.
  • Add tags to assets so they are easy to find later.

Workflow Efficiency

  • Use the Duplicate function (⋮ menu) on posts, tasks, and events to save time on recurring content.
  • Set default team members when creating a project — all deliverables in the project will be pre-assigned to that Designer, Copywriter, and Publisher.
  • Use the Calendar view on the Dashboard to spot content gaps and scheduling conflicts at a glance.
  • Check the Publishing Queue daily if you are a publisher — items waiting there are approved and ready to go live.

Communication

  • Make sure your Slack account details are up to date in your user profile so you receive Slack notifications correctly.
  • When assigning deliverables, add clear reference images and design specs to avoid back-and-forth with your designer.
  • Use the task description field to add context, links, or brief details rather than messaging separately.

Common Questions

I can't see a brand in the dropdown. What's wrong?

The brand may be deactivated or you may not be assigned to that region. Ask your Admin to check brand and region assignments in Settings.

The publish button isn't working for a platform.

The platform integration may not be connected or the token may have expired. Check Settings → Integrations or contact your Admin.

I'm not receiving Slack notifications.

Ensure your Slack username is configured in your user profile. Also check that the Slack integration is active in Settings. If the issue persists, contact your Admin.

How do I reset my password?

Contact your Administrator. They can reset your password in Settings → Users.
